moment of creation · animation toolkit

A toolkit for two animations — a pure-CSS First and a binary Last — combined into a Together. Left: the First, looping. Middle: the Together, the two combined. Right: the Last. Sculpt with 40 abstract knobs: the First explodes six ways (the Blast — cone, shockwave, shards, lightning, nova) and the Last resolves into eight neon Forms (ring, polygon, star, slit, lens, lattice, spiral, cross) framed by slices of light — not a field of tiny particles. Randomize either half, Swap their order, or chain up to ten and drag the links to reorder. 40 parameters and 12 presets drive every window live. Source & engine →

Each window is the one overlay played in a mode (first / last / together) — the same source the npm run export single files use. The page conducts all three: Swap plays the Together Last→First; the chain plays every Together through the middle while the sides show the selected link's two halves (drag a link to reorder). The knobs are abstract, pedal-style sculptors — the Blast that shapes the explosion, the Form the merger resolves into, slices of light, and a signed Dusk that ends on a collapse or a burst — drawn as snappy neon vectors. Controls + presets come from one source (dials.json / presets.json).
